Visual illusion lab: meetings

Sign up for one-on-one meeting with course staff

Please sign up for at least one 15 minute slot to discuss your illusion project ideas with one of the course staff. You should have ideas for a scientific question ready to discuss and an initial design/sketch of your illusion. If you can't make any of the listed times, please email your TAs.
